# Behavioral Quiz Preparation

Anthropic cares deeply about culture fit, values, and how you think about AI safety.

## Key Themes Anthropic Cares About

Based on their mission and job postings:

1. **Safety-first mindset**: Do you think about risks proactively?
2. **Intellectual honesty**: Can you admit uncertainty?
3. **Collaboration**: Do you work well with others?
4. **Communication**: Can you explain complex ideas?
5. **Long-term thinking**: Do you consider second-order effects?
6. **Mission alignment**: Why AI safety? Why Anthropic?

---

## The STAR Method

For behavioral questions, structure answers as:

- **S**ituation: Context and background
- **T**ask: What needed to be done
- **A**ction: What you specifically did
- **R**esult: What happened, what you learned

Keep answers to 2-3 minutes.

---

## STAR Stories to Prepare

Prepare stories for these themes:

### 1. Technical Challenge
*"Tell me about a technically challenging project."*

Your story should show:
- Deep technical work
- Problem-solving process
- How you overcame obstacles
- What you learned

### 2. Collaboration
*"Tell me about working with a difficult team member."*

Your story should show:
- Communication skills
- Empathy and patience
- Finding common ground
- Positive outcome

### 3. Failure/Learning
*"Tell me about a time you failed."*

Your story should show:
- Honest acknowledgment
- What went wrong
- What you learned
- How you improved

### 4. Safety/Ethics
*"Tell me about a time you raised a safety concern."*

Your story should show:
- Proactive thinking
- Willingness to speak up
- How you communicated risk
- Resolution

### 5. Ambiguity
*"Tell me about working with unclear requirements."*

Your story should show:
- Comfort with uncertainty
- How you gathered information
- Decision-making process
- Iteration

## "Why Anthropic?" Essay

Write a genuine answer. They can tell if you're just saying what they want to hear.

### Template

**Paragraph 1: Your AI safety journey**
- When did you start caring about AI safety?
- What experiences shaped your views?

**Paragraph 2: Why Anthropic specifically**
- What do you admire about their approach?
- Which research has resonated with you?
- Why them over other AI labs?

**Paragraph 3: What you'd contribute**
- What unique skills/perspective do you bring?
- What do you want to work on?
- How does this fit your career goals?

### Tips
- Be specific (name papers, products, values)
- Be genuine (don't just say what you think they want)
- Show you've done research
- Connect to your own work/projects

## Common Behavioral Questions

### About Your Background
- Walk me through your resume
- What's the most interesting project you've worked on?
- Why did you leave your last role?

### About Technical Work
- Describe a technical decision you made that you're proud of
- Tell me about a time you had to learn something new quickly
- How do you approach debugging?

### About Collaboration
- How do you handle disagreements on technical decisions?
- Tell me about giving/receiving feedback
- How do you work with non-technical stakeholders?

### About AI/Safety
- What do you see as the biggest risks from AI?
- How do you think about AI safety in your work?
- What's your view on [current AI topic]?

### About Anthropic
- Why Anthropic?
- What do you know about our research?
- Where do you see AI going in 5 years?

## Questions to Ask Them

Prepare 3-5 thoughtful questions:

### About the Role
- What does success look like in the first 6 months?
- What's the biggest challenge the team is facing?
- How much autonomy do people have in choosing projects?

### About the Team
- How does the team collaborate with research vs engineering?
- What's the culture like for giving/receiving feedback?
- How do you handle disagreements about safety vs capabilities?

### About Anthropic
- How has the company culture evolved as you've grown?
- What's Anthropic's approach to publishing research?
- How do you balance commercial and safety work?

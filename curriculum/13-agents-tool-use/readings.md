# Agents & Tool Use Readings

## Core Papers (Required)

### 1. ReAct: Synergizing Reasoning and Acting in Language Models
- **Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2210.03629
- **Authors:** Yao et al.
- **Year:** 2022
- **Difficulty:** 3/5
- **Time:** 1.5 hours
- **Why read:** The foundational pattern for modern agents

**Key ideas:**
- Interleave "Thought:" and "Action:" steps
- Use observations to inform next steps
- Compare to Chain-of-Thought and Action-only baselines

### 2. Toolformer: Language Models Can Teach Themselves to Use Tools
- **Link:** https://arxiv.org/abs/2302.04761
- **Authors:** Schick et al. (Meta)
- **Year:** 2023
- **Difficulty:** 4/5
- **Time:** 2 hours
- **Why read:** Self-supervised tool learning

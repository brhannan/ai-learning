# Interview Preparation

This section helps you prepare for Anthropic-style technical interviews.

## Interview Structure (Based on Research)

Anthropic interviews typically include:

1. **Initial Screen**: Recruiter call, basic background
2. **Technical Screen**: Coding + ML fundamentals
3. **System Design**: ML system design problem
4. **Paper Discussion**: Analyze and discuss a research paper
5. **Behavioral**: Culture fit, values alignment

## Key Anthropic Interview Tips

Based on interview guides and job postings:

### They Value
- **Direct evidence of ability** over credentials
- **Safety-first thinking** in technical decisions
- **Communication skills** - can you explain complex ideas?
- **Independent research** - have you done interesting work?
- **Intellectual honesty** - can you admit what you don't know?

### They Look For
- Deep ML fundamentals (transformers, optimization)
- Understanding of alignment challenges
- Systems thinking for infrastructure
- Passion for AI safety
- Collaborative mindset

### What to Prepare
1. **Your projects**: Be ready to discuss in detail
2. **Anthropic's research**: Know their major papers
3. **"Why Anthropic"**: Have a specific, genuine answer
4. **Technical fundamentals**: Attention, RLHF, scaling
5. **Failure modes**: What can go wrong with AI systems?

## Before Applying

Make sure you have:
- [ ] 3+ portfolio projects on GitHub
- [ ] At least one blog post
- [ ] Deep familiarity with 3+ Anthropic papers
- [ ] Practice with system design
- [ ] Prepared STAR stories
- [ ] Written "Why Anthropic" essay
